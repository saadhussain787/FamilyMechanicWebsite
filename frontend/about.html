Who We Are

Our Story & Philosophy

Family-Owned & Operated

Dependable Car Care Grounded in Honesty

At My Choice Auto Service, we believe that bringing your vehicle in for repair shouldn't be a stressful experience. Founded as a family-owned shop, our mission has always been simple: to provide honest, straightforward, and highly skilled mechanical care directly to our local community.

Led by Shafiq Patel, a Certified Auto Technician, our shop combines decades of hands-on diagnostic experience with modern equipment. Unlike large dealerships, you deal directly with the mechanics working on your car. We take the time to explain every diagnosis clearly, outlining what needs immediate attention and what can wait.

Whether you visit us at Unit 5 on Select Ave for a routine oil change or complex electrical troubleshooting, you can count on transparent pricing, OEM-quality parts, and a dedication to getting you back on the road safely.
